Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BundleVue.

What is BundleVue?
BundleVue is a mobile app that helps you track your data usage — both for your personal bundle and for shared group bundles. See how much data you and your family members have used, in real time.
Which platforms is BundleVue available on?
BundleVue is available for both iOS (iPhone) and Android. You can mix platforms within a group — for example, one person on iPhone and another on Android.
How does BundleVue measure my data usage?
BundleVue reads your device's built-in data counters. It does not intercept your traffic or use a VPN. This means it works with any carrier — no setup or login with your provider needed.
What are group bundles?
Many mobile plans share a data allowance across multiple SIM cards. With BundleVue, you can create a group that mirrors this shared bundle. Every member sees their own usage and the group total — so you always know who's using what.
Is BundleVue free?
Personal usage tracking is free. Group bundles and advanced features like group notifications require BundleVue Premium — available as a monthly, yearly, or lifetime subscription.
Is my data safe?
Yes. BundleVue only measures how much data your device uses — it cannot see what you browse or which apps you use. Cloud data is stored securely in the EU. You can delete all your cloud data at any time from within the app.
Can I use BundleVue without an account?
Yes. You can track your personal data usage without signing in. An account is only needed for cloud sync and group features. You can sign in with your Apple or Google account.
Does BundleVue work with any carrier?
Yes. BundleVue is completely carrier-independent. It works with any mobile provider — no carrier login, API, or special setup required.
