Privacy Choices

Last updated: March 20, 2026

BundleVue does not track you or sell your data. Your individual usage data stays on your device. This page explains what choices you have regarding any data we do store.

Your options

Delete your account and data

You can delete your BundleVue account and all associated data (group data, authentication record) directly from within the app via Settings → Delete account. This is permanent and cannot be undone.

Delete local data

Your personal usage history, bundle configuration and notification settings are stored locally on your device. You can clear this data via Settings → Clear all data, or by deleting and reinstalling the app.

Request a copy of your data

To request a copy of the data we hold about you in our backend (group membership, display name, usage totals), email us at hello@bundlevue.app. We will respond within 30 days.

Opt out of crash reporting

Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics) are sent automatically to help us fix bugs. These are not linked to your identity. At this time, there is no in-app toggle for crash reporting. If you wish to opt out, contact us at hello@bundlevue.app.

No tracking, no ads

BundleVue does not use advertising identifiers, does not track you across apps or websites, and does not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Questions

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at hello@bundlevue.app. We are based in the Netherlands and subject to GDPR.
