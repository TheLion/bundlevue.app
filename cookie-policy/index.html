Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

We currently only use essential cookies. No analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking cookies.

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how MJ Consultancy B.V. ("we", "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on the bundlevue.app website. This policy complies with the Dutch Telecommunicatiewet (Telecommunication Act) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR / AVG).

This policy applies to the website at https://bundlevue.app. The BundleVue iOS app does not use cookies — for app-related data processing, see our Privacy Policy.

2. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They help the website function properly, remember your preferences, and provide information to the website owner.

3. Cookies we use

3.1 Essential cookies (no consent required)

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. Under the Dutch Telecommunicatiewet, essential cookies do not require consent.

CookiePurposeDuration
Session cookiesMaintain website functionality during your visitSession (deleted when you close your browser)

We keep essential cookies to a minimum and only use them when technically necessary.

3.2 Non-essential cookies

We currently do not use any non-essential cookies. Specifically:

  • No analytics cookies (no Google Analytics, no Matomo, etc.)
  • No advertising or tracking cookies
  • No social media cookies
  • No third-party marketing cookies

If we introduce non-essential cookies in the future, we will:

  1. Update this Cookie Policy
  2. Implement a cookie consent banner before placing any non-essential cookies
  3. Only place non-essential cookies after you have given explicit, informed consent

4. Newsletter signup

Our website includes a newsletter signup form. When you submit your email address:

  • We collect your email address based on your consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a))
  • You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in every newsletter email
  • The newsletter signup itself may involve cookies set by the form provider — these will only be placed with your consent if they are non-essential

5. Third-party cookies

We currently do not load any third-party services on our website that place cookies. If this changes (for example, if we embed videos, add analytics, or integrate social media), we will update this policy and implement appropriate consent mechanisms.

6. Your consent and choices

Under Dutch law (Telecommunicatiewet)

  • Essential cookies may be placed without consent — they are necessary for the website to work
  • Non-essential cookies require your prior, informed consent before they can be placed
  • You must be able to withdraw consent as easily as you gave it

How to manage cookies in your browser

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Here are links for common browsers:

  • Safari: https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/
  • Chrome: https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647
  • Firefox: https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer
  • Edge: https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge

Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of the website.

7. Cookie banner

Since we currently only use essential cookies, a cookie consent banner is not required for existing functionality. However, should we add any non-essential cookies in the future, we will implement a cookie banner that:

  • Clearly explains what cookies are used and for what purpose
  • Allows you to accept or reject non-essential cookies before they are placed
  • Allows you to change your preferences at any time
  • Does not use pre-ticked checkboxes
  • Does not block access to the website if you decline non-essential cookies

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If we start using non-essential cookies, we will notify you and ask for your consent.
